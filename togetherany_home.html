About this project

What is this site?

This site has been created for the international student exchange between russian and german schools. Such exchanges had been being conducted before 2020.That year people all over the world had to stay home with no way to go abroad. Realizing the pandemic realities, in the second half of 2021 our team began working on a project that would help to arrange a remote exchange program. This site is dedicated to the 2022 exchange and it's possible that it'll be completed for the future ones. The participants of this program are the Secondary School ‚Ññ45 of Saint-Petersburg's Primorskiy district and GSBSchule Bergedorf Hamburg. This site is dedicated to the events our school participates in.

What can you find here?

This site contains information about events our school participates in. Here, you can find content created during this events, a space for communication and activities and information about how you can join our project, about various events in our city and universities of our city. Besides, here you can find materials that will help you to become accostumed to Saint-Petersburg's culture, to various school-related materials for lessons.

What parts does the project consist of?

First, there is the technical component that is being represented by an Internet-site created with Google.sites certain someone's hands ‚úã ü§ö, Padlet virtual board, virtual Telegram bot-companion, Telegram channel created to discuss interesting or concerning cultural aspects of one's life in the participants' countries, or just news. Second, there is the educational program featuring several virtual quests around Saint-Petersburg, a selection of quizes about russian culture and history. Third, the communicational part is being realized by video conferences and discussions within the Internet resources.
